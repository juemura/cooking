INSTRUCTIONS

1. Wash 1 cup of rice.

2. Cut the chicken into cubes.

3. Marinate the chicken with a pich of salt and pepper.

4. Get a pan and put it over the stove on high heat.

5. Put a teaspoon of oil on the pan.

6. Place the chicken into the pan and when it is about medium rare dump some of the rice into the frying pan.

7. Then dump some vegetables into the frying pan.

8. Crack the eggs into the pan.

9. Stir-fry the rice until every ingredient is cooked.

10. Get a plate and serve.
